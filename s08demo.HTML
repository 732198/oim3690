Alaska — Suggested 7-Day Tour

A compact 7-day loop highlighting coastal wildlife, glaciers, Denali, and northern lights opportunities.

Day 1 — Anchorage

Arrive in Anchorage, explore downtown and the Tony Knowles Coastal Trail. Grab fresh seafood.

Day 2 — Seward / Kenai Fjords

Boat tour to see tidewater glaciers and marine wildlife (whales, sea otters, puffins).

Day 3 — Homer / Kachemak Bay

Coastal views, clamming, local art, and the Homer Spit.

Day 4 — Travel north toward Denali

Overnight in Talkeetna or nearby; consider flightseeing for glacier views.

Day 5 — Denali National Park

Bus tours, short hikes, and wildlife viewing in Denali.

Day 6 — Fairbanks & Northern Lights

Higher chance of aurora viewing (seasonal); guided tours recommended.

Day 7 — Flightseeing / Glacier visit

Flightseeing over glaciers or boat trips; return to Anchorage for departure.
