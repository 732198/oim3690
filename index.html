Alaska ‚Äî Suggested 7-Day Tour

A compact 7-day loop highlighting coastal wildlife, glaciers, Denali, and northern lights opportunities. Replace photos with your own if desired.

Day 1 ‚Äî Anchorage

Arrive in Anchorage, explore downtown and the Tony Knowles Coastal Trail. Grab fresh seafood and rest up.

Day 2 ‚Äî Seward / Kenai Fjords

Take a boat tour to see tidewater glaciers and marine wildlife (whales, sea otters, puffins). Bring layers and a waterproof jacket.

Day 3 ‚Äî Homer / Kachemak Bay

Drive or take a ferry to Homer for coastal views, clamming, and local art. Try local halibut and visit the Homer Spit.

Day 4 ‚Äî Travel north to Denali region

Head north toward Denali (overnight in Talkeetna or nearby). Consider a small-plane flightseeing trip for glacier views.

Day 5 ‚Äî Denali National Park

Spend the day in Denali: bus tours into the park, short hikes, and wildlife viewing. Keep distance from animals and follow park guidance.

Day 6 ‚Äî Fairbanks & Northern Lights

Travel to Fairbanks area for a higher chance of aurora viewing (seasonal). Consider a guided aurora tour and warm layers.

Day 7 ‚Äî Flightseeing / Glacier visit

Finish with a flightseeing tour over glaciers or a boat trip (seasonal). Return to Anchorage and prepare for departure.
