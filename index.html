From Beijing to Babson

This site is a living record of my life story. I was born in Beijing, China, moved to the United States in third grade, and grew up shaped by music, sports, and the experience of building a life across cultures.

I played violin, competed in tennis, started college at Northeastern, spent my first semester traveling around Europe beginning in the Czech Republic, and later transferred to Babson.

Over time, I will expand this website with full stories, photos, and reflections.
