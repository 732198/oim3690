## Key takeaway

AI works best as a coding assistant, not a decision maker. The idea, the content, and the purpose of the site has to come from you first — then AI can translate it into code. The write → prompt → paste → commit → push workflow is fast once the tools are set up.

## What surprised me

How quickly a real, publicly accessible website can go live. Writing two or three sentences about myself on paper and feeding them into an AI produced a working site in minutes.
